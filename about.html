About AI Pilot

AI Pilot is a focused AI SEO tools directory for content teams, niche site builders, affiliate publishers, SEO freelancers, and ecommerce operators.

The site intentionally avoids becoming a broad AI tools list. The primary niche is AI SEO: content optimization, keyword research, SERP analysis, technical audits, internal linking, WordPress SEO, and AI search visibility. Ecommerce SEO is the adjacent subtopic because product pages, category pages, structured data, reviews, and support questions naturally connect to SEO workflows.

What We Publish

We publish tool directories, tool comparison pages, workflow articles, ecommerce SEO guides, and source-backed articles. The goal is to help readers choose useful tools and build repeatable workflows, not to publish generic AI hype.

How The Site Can Earn Revenue

AI Pilot may earn revenue from affiliate links, sponsored placements, templates, and consulting. Commercial relationships should not decide editorial recommendations. Paid placements should be labeled clearly.
