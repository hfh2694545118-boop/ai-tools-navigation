# AI SEO Primary Niche Keyword Plan

This plan keeps the website focused on one commercial niche: AI SEO tools. Ecommerce SEO remains the adjacent subtopic because product pages, collections, reviews, FAQs, and structured data naturally connect to AI SEO workflows.

## Strategy

- Primary niche: AI SEO tools, AI SEO software, AI content optimization tools.
- Adjacent niche: AI ecommerce SEO tools and product-page optimization.
- Audience: niche site owners, SaaS content teams, affiliate publishers, Shopify and WooCommerce sellers, SEO freelancers, and small businesses.
- Monetization: SEO tool affiliate links, sponsored placements, downloadable templates, consulting, and comparison-page sponsorships.
- Page priority: tool comparison pages first, workflow articles second, glossary or generic AI content last.

## Content Rules

- Every article must target AI SEO, ecommerce SEO, AI content optimization, AI search visibility, WordPress SEO, keyword research, internal linking, or technical SEO.
- Remove broad AI topics that do not support the niche.
- Every article must include real data, a concrete case, source links, a practical workflow, and a warning against thin AI-generated content.
- Tool pages must include who the tool is for, who it is not for, free or trial limits when known, real use cases, source links, alternative tools, and affiliate disclosure.
